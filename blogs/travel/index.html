ABOUT
Daniela

I've always dreamed of exploring the world, I've been wanting to do this since I was about 12. My family has always been big on travel, whether it was to visit relatives scattered across North America, a road trip to Alberta, or even a camping trip. Once I got a little older, I always jumped on every opportunity my school had to offer to travel, and I joined 3 exchange trips to France, Spain, and Germany. Unfortunately, the last one was cancelled due to COVID. Now that I've completed my high school career, I'm taking this trip to learn about all the distinct European cultures and to grow as a person, hopefully finding what I want to study in university.

ABOUT
Will

While I've been able to travel certain parts of the word with my family, I had never gotten the chance to see the whole of Europe - or even the whole of England! Now that twelve long years of school are over, I'm taking this trip to gain some independence and really see a large portion of the world, before heading off for another four years of school - and who knows - maybe it will turn into eight or even twelve total years. So far, I've already learned more, and experienced way more than I did in high school, and I can't wait to continue to broaden my horizons, grow as a person, and then bring all of those new skills back to my university career and future tech career.
